Our goal is to connect small dollar donors with fundraisers for marginalized individuals and families. Find people who need the $5 you didn't spend on coffee today and make their lives better.

All fundraisers are vetted to the best of our ability, but we cannot make guarantees about their validity. If you find a fundraiser that appears broken or that you believe is a scam, please report it. For more information and the full terms and conditions, please read the F.A.Q.
